Support Staff

The chancellor's support staff manages the daily requirements of the office, including communications, contacts, calendar, travel, public outreach, conflict resolution, research and other professional activities. The staff ensures integration of UCLA's goals and priorities, and works with the chancellor to coordinate his many appointments with students, faculty, staff, university constituents and community leaders.
