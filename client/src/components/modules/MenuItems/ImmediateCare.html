Scheduling an Immediate Care Video Visit does not connect you to your PCP or specialist.
If you have questions regarding a recent visit, test results, or referrals, please contact the office of your provider.
What's available through Immediate Care Video Visits:
  • - Acute illness evaluation (e.g., colds, fevers, flu and flu-like symptoms, urinary tract infections, rash, pink eye, etc.)
  • - Acute pain or minor injury evaluation (e.g., sprains, strains, etc.)
  • - Short term refill of non-controlled medications
What's not available through Immediate Care Video Visits:
  • - Chronic disease management (e.g., cholesterol management, diabetes management, etc.)
  • - Chronic pain management
  • - Refills of controlled substances (controlled substances include, but not limited to: Alprazolam, Clonazepam, Hydrocodone, Codeine, Oxycodone, Tramadol, Amphetamines, and Methylphenidate)
